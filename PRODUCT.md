# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Small business owners and local brand operators — cafes, tradies, salons, retail, professional services. They are time-poor, don't speak tech, and are spending money they've earned. They want proof: proof you can be trusted, proof the quality is real, proof they won't get burned by another bad web agency.

## Product Purpose

SanjStudio is a custom website studio specialising in small businesses and local brands. The site must convert skeptical, budget-conscious business owners who have seen too many generic web agencies. Success is a contact form submission from someone who feels like they found the real thing.

## Brand Personality

Engineered. Trusted. Precise. — the authority of a specialist studio, the approachability of a local expert.

## Anti-references

Wix and Squarespace builder ad aesthetics. Fiverr-gig portfolios. Template showcases that look identical to every other web agency site. Cluttered "we do everything" agency homepages. Fake urgency and hype copy.

## Design Principles

1. Practice what you preach — the site itself is the product demonstration; its quality IS the pitch
2. Precision signals trust — surgical layout, deliberate whitespace, zero decoration without function
3. Show don't tell — real work examples over marketing claims
4. Earned restraint — every element earns its place; nothing added for visual noise
5. Approachable authority — expert and precise, never cold or corporate

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Readable for non-technical audiences. Clean hierarchy that works without colour.
